Explore his discography on Spotify or YouTube before the concert to hype yourself. With hits like "Poonakaalu Loading" and "Bheemla Nayak," Ram Miriyala’s music transcends language and resonates with fans worldwide. Get ready to sing along to your favorite tracks live!

DJ DEEPIKA

Feel the Beats with DJ Deepika South India's No. 1 DJ!

Get ready to dance the night away with DJ Deepika , South India’s top-ranked DJ, known for her electrifying mixes and high-energy performances. With a reputation for setting the stage on fire, DJ Deepika has earned accolades across the country for her unique ability to blend beats that keep audiences on their feet. Whether it’s the latest chartbusters or classic favorites, her sets are always a crowd-puller, making her the perfect addition to the RAM Miriyala Concert & Carnival.

Having headlined major music festivals and performed at premier events, DJ Deepika’s talent spans genres, from Bollywood and EDM to regional hits. Her expertise and passion for music ensure an unforgettable experience for all attendees. Don’t miss her thrilling performance as she takes the stage at the Viswanadh Sports Club , adding an extra dose of excitement to the carnival vibes. Prepare to lose yourself in the rhythm and join the party like never before!
